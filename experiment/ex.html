Attention
The patches that segment just right, are difficult to get them to clump, or under segment.
Whereas the ones that clump easily, it is difficult to under segment them.
Sometimes the "clumped" will look "just right" in some places, and at the same time the "just right" will be clumped.
There really is no way to get it just right.
Therefore, please use discretion when using the experimental data in this section.
Look at the patches, compare for yourself, and use what you feel looks right.
